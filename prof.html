Project: Guam Environmental Reporting System; Client: EPA

The Water Air Land Territorial Electronic Record System (W.A.L.T.E.R.S) is a spatially linked electronic document storage system. WALTERS is able to identify facilities, sites, or places of environmental interest in Guam. Supported development of tools to enhance the functionality of the application. Assisted in testing software and resolving any bugs in the system architecture.

Project: Water Assessment Tool; Client: EPA

to develop a database and front end for management of questions to be used for assessments of drinking water and wastewater infrastructure during emergency response and recovery. The system will support a set of editable questions, logic to relate to questions, selection of geographic area of interest and primary inventory of label(s) of sites to be assessed during specific responses. The system will support automatic generation of survey’s using ArcGIS Survey123. This project will incorporate database design and creation using Django, GIS Scripting using Survey123, as well as interactive mapping.
